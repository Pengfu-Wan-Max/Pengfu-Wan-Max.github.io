About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Industrial Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Gangyan Xu. Now, I am a visiting student at the Smith School of Business, Queen’s University, Canada, and I am fortunate to get the supervision under Prof. Jue Wang. My current research focuses on learning-based decision-making, especially reinforcement learning for optimization problems, with applications in emergency management, intelligent transportation, and wildlife operations research.

Research Interests

  • Emergency management; UAV-based applications; intelligent transportation optimization
  • Sustainability; wildlife operations research
